# **App Name**: Fit Domination

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Impactful, dark green hero section with a white title, subtitle, and prominent CTA to immediately capture user attention.
- Visual Impact Section: Vertically laid out blocks with dark green backgrounds, minimalist white icons, and concise text to create a sense of progression.
- Category Cards: Premium cards for categories like 'Fit Breakfast,' 'Zero-Sugar Desserts,' and 'Protein-Packed Air Fryer Recipes,' each with a dark green background, soft border, illustrative image, and highlighted category name.
- App Experience Showcase: Dark blocks with light icons and subtle animations on scroll to highlight the app's organization, power, and simplicity.
- Final CTA: Dark green background with a white or lighter green button, featuring a large, central CTA for a strong closing impression.

## Style Guidelines:

- Base color: Dark green (#006400) as the primary background to convey power and control.
- Text and Highlights: White (#FFFFFF) used for contrast and to emphasize key elements.
- Depth: An even darker shade of green (#003300) to create depth and a premium feel.
- Font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for a modern and strong look. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Mobile-first, fully responsive, fluid, and modern design, optimized for smaller screens with an immersive layout and well-defined contrasts.
- Subtle animations such as fade-in and vertical slides on scroll to create a sense of visual 'unlocking.'
